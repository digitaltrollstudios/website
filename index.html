About Us

What we do and what we are

Creating TTRPGs since 2022

We are team of students creating a tabletop role-playing game

  • Over 10 combined years of experience

DTS was established as part of the STARTUP@TBZ school project and is run as a "Einfache Gesellschaft". The project is limited until 09.06.2023, but the learners are free to continue it on their own responsibility.
